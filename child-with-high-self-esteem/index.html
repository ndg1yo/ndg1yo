Child with high self-esteem

As discussed in the previous post of people with high esteem, the characteristics of people with high were listed and now we will look child with high self-esteem through and through in this post.

What about the level of self-esteem of your child? Is your child a child with high or low self-esteem? Is it a high child? Take a closer look at the characteristics of a child with high self-esteem and think about what kind of your child is.

A child with high self-esteem has following characteristics. If your child has more than 6 characteristics below, you can think of him or her as having high-esteem overall.

No fear to have relationships with other people

They dont have any fear of having relationships with other people. Children with high self-esteem are not afraid to talk to new people because they are confident that other people will also like themselves.

Empathy and consideration

Self-respect can also lead to others. They already know that the relationship through empathy and consideration is very stable. It is because they learned the attitude or method of maintaining a good relationship through the successful relationships they have been through.

Kind and polite

They are kind and polite. They learn how to communicate with others by seeing what other people are doing to them. They learn the kind and polite aspects they have received, and they do it same.

Satisfaction

They know how to be satisfied with their achievements. Basically children do not overconfident or underestimate their abilities. Children with well-developed self-esteem know how to be purely happy with the achievements they have achieved. Satisfaction with these achievements become the energy to challenge new things.

New things

They have confidence and try new things well. They know how to be happy and satisfied with his achievements. A feeling of joy makes them not afraid to take on other challenges. they feel that challenging a new thing itself is not a frightening thing but a joyful thing that gives you another joy.

Passionate

They try to do things proactively and proactively. When they do things, they don't act passively and passively. They always try to be proactive and take the initiative.

Positive way of thinking

They are not good at negative thoughts. They focus on the current situation. What if this doesn't work? What if this goes wrong? Instead of being restless, they enjoy and focus on the current situation. Even if they have negative thoughts, they think they can quickly come up with a countermeasure or try harder.

Positive words

They don't use pessimistic words well and words that deny reality or pessimistic words. They express themselves with positive words.

Responsibility

They put the responsibility of work on themselves and think that they are responsible for doing something. If they are making a big house out of blocks and it doesn't work well, they think that they will just need a little more and keep challenging or they guess they tried to make it too big. Then they will think "shall we change it a little bit more?"

Never frustrated

They are not very frustrated if they fail something. Even if they fail and suffer frustration, they won't get hurt very much. They just think of ways to overcome failures. Because they know that with a little more effort, they can experience other successes.
